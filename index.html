Introduction

Multilevel factor

Except the "miles per gallon", there might exsits some relationship among the left items, we consider a multilevel factor level. We guess that the "drat" and "qsec" might are linear dependent on "disp", "hp", and "wt". So, we need the 2 levels.

\[Y_1=\beta_{10}+\beta_{11}X_{1}+\beta_{12}X_2+\beta_{13}X_3,\] \[Y_2=\beta_{20}+\beta_{21}X_{1}+\beta_{22}X_2+\beta_{23}X_3,\] where

  1. \(Y_1\) is "drat", \(Y_2\) is "qsec";

  2. \(X_{1}\) is "disp", \(X_{2}\) is "hp", \(X_{3}\) is "wt";

  3. \(\beta_{ij}\) are the coefficients.
